Who I am
About Me

I started web development back in 2017 and have been building production applications professionally since then. My main focus is backend development with Laravel and PHP, combined with Vue.js on the frontend and deep experience in e-commerce platforms like PrestaShop and WordPress/WooCommerce.

Over the years I've worked on everything from small business landing pages to complex multi-tenant SaaS platforms, custom ERP/CRM systems, marketplace backends, and high-traffic e-commerce stores. I care deeply about code quality, documentation, and clean architecture — every project I deliver is something I'm proud to put my name on.

I'm a remote-first developer — I've been working remotely with international clients since the beginning of my career. I understand what it means to be a reliable remote team member: proactive communication, daily updates, transparent time tracking, and no surprises.

Outside of work I contribute to open-source, keep up with the Laravel ecosystem, and occasionally write about development. I'm based in Lviv, Ukraine — UTC+2/+3, with good overlap for European clients and flexible hours for North American teams.
